La Brea

A mysterious sinkhole in Los Angeles called La Brea, opens and swallows up various characters from different walks of life. These characters find themselves in a prehistoric world filled with dangerous creatures and unfamiliar landscapes. The show explores their struggle for survival, the challenges they face in this strange environment, and the mysteries surrounding the nature of the sinkhole itself.
